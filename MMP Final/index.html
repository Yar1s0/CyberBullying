What is Cyber-Bulling?

Cyberbullying is bullying that takes place over digital devices like cell phones, computers, and tablets. Cyberbullying can occur through SMS, Text, and apps, or online in social media, forums, or gaming where people can view, participate in, or share content. Cyberbullying includes sending, posting, or sharing negative, harmful, false, or mean content about someone else. It can include sharing personal or private information about someone else causing embarrassment or humiliation. Some cyberbullying crosses the line into unlawful or criminal behavior.

The most common places where cyberbullying occurs are:

  • Social Media, such as Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, and Twitter
  • SMS (Short Message Service) also known as Text Message sent through devices
  • Instant Message (via devices, email provider services, apps, and social media messaging features)
  • Email

Who we are?

This page was created with the purpose of helping who , in a way or another, have suffered from Cyber-Bullying. The goal is to share our stories with people who have lived the same as us but in a anonymous way. The main goal behind this idea is give to those who are going through this bad experience a way to be able to vent their pain and from that help each other to heal and be able to move on and forgive those who have done us wrong.
